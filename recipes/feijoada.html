FEIJOADA

Feijoada is prepared with black beans, a variety of pork or beef, bacon, smoked pork ribs. The final dish has the beans and meat pieces barely covered by a dark purplish-brown broth.

The taste is strong. It is customary to serve it with fried collards and white rice. As a celebratory dish, feijoada is intended to be enjoyed throughout the day and not eaten under rushed circumstances.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram of black bean;
  • Water;
  • 2 beef tongues jerky;
  • 1 kilogram of jerked beef;
  • 1/2 kilogram of pork ribs;
  • 1/2 kilogram of pork loin;
  • 1/2 kilogram of pepperoni sausage;
  • 3 onions;
  • 3 cloves of garlic;
  • 1/2 cup of lard.

Steps

  1. Soak the beans for 12 hours;
  2. Wash the salted meat well, cut it into pieces and soak it in water for 12 hours, changing the water at least twice;
  3. Put the beans to cook in plenty of water;
  4. In a separate pan, cook the salted meats for 30 minutes, drain the water and set aside;
  5. When the beans are starting to soften, add the meats, the sausage and simmer over low heat until everything is well cooked (takes roughly 3 hours);
  6. Heat the lard for 15 minutes
  7. Place the heated lard in the beans, mash them well and let it boil for 30 minutes;
  8. Add onions and cloves of garlic to the beans;
  9. Serve very hot accompanied if you wish by finely fried collard, boiled yellow cassava and white rice.
